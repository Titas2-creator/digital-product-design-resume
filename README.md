## About Me

I am a first-year Bachelor of Design (B.Des.) student in Digital Product Design at **École Intuit Lab × Techno India University**. My foundation lies in traditional art, illustration, printmaking, and visual communication, and I am currently exploring **UI/UX design**, **human-centred design**, and **digital experiences**.

I enjoy shaping ideas from observation to composition, then turning them into experiences that feel clear, thoughtful, and visually engaging.
